Winston Wolfe Holdings LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates BrokeHappens.com (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information in accordance with applicable U.S. federal and state privacy laws.

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access and disclosure.

BrokeHappens.com is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.
